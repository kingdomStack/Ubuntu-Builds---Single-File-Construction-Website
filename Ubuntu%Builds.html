Who We Are

Building with purpose since 2010

Ubuntu Builds was founded on the principle of 'Ubuntu' - I am because we are. We believe that great construction isn't just about bricks and mortar; it's about building communities, creating opportunities, and transforming spaces where people live, work, and thrive.

Since our establishment in 2010, we've grown from a small team with a big vision to become one of Johannesburg's most trusted construction companies.

Our philosophy centers on quality craftsmanship, transparent communication, and lasting relationships with our clients and community.

Our Story

2010

Foundation

Ubuntu Builds was founded by Samuel Khumalo with a small team of 5 dedicated builders, operating from a single office in Johannesburg.

2014

Expansion

Expanded services to include commercial construction and secured first major commercial project - a retail center in Sandton.

2018

Certification

Achieved CIDB 7GB and 7CE grading, ISO 9001 certification, and grew team to 50+ skilled professionals.

2023

Present Day

Now one of Johannesburg's leading construction firms with 75+ professionals and 500+ completed projects across Gauteng.

Our Mission

To deliver exceptional construction services that build communities, create opportunities, and exceed client expectations through quality craftsmanship and the Ubuntu philosophy.

Our Vision

To be Johannesburg's most trusted construction partner, recognized for transforming spaces and building sustainable communities through innovative and ethical practices.

Integrity
